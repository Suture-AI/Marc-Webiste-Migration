Producing, selling, advertising, distributing, or possessing even a single image of child pornography can lead to facing severe charges.

Depending upon the circumstances, child pornography can be prosecuted at the state or federal level.

Since the graveness of the situation is too tough to handle alone, you need an entire legal team by your side to avoid conviction. That's why you shouldn't waste another minute to consult with a San Diego child pornography attorney.

When charged with such an offense, the court is less likely to be sympathetic to your case. So, not only are the penalties severe, but the social implications can follow you for a lifetime of embarrassment.

The penalties for child pornography charges range from a misdemeanor to a felony.

If charged with a misdemeanor possession offense, you can face a $1,000 fine and up to 1 year in county jail.

In comparison, a felony charge can lead to a state prison sentence with a fine of up to $100,000.

Regardless of being charged with a misdemeanor or felony, you'll be liable to register as a sex offender for life. In turn, this can lead to a continuous social stigma which can be difficult to get through.
